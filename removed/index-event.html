Venue

Classy location in the heart of the city.

UI Conf 2017 will be taking place at Koko Club London, very central location on London Street, the city's most famous thoroughfare which runs, much as it has for more than three centuries, from Piata Victoriei in the north of the city all the way down to Piata Natiunilor Unite and the Dambovita river.
